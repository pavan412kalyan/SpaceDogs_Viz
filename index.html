The viz shows the rockets and all the dogs that participated in all the missions between the years 1951 and 1958. The dogs are connected to Rockets in which they participated by red lines and the dogs that traveled together by green lines(more the luminance, more the frequency). Number of black dots on the image of dog represents total missions by it. The legends are mentioned below. Hover on the dogs/rockets for more details on the left side.
